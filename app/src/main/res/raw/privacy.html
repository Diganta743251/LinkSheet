LinkMaster Privacy Policy

🔒 NO DATA COLLECTION

All links are stored locally on your device. LinkMaster does not collect, transmit, or store any of your browsing data on external servers.

📱 What We Don't Collect

  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Personal information
  • ❌ Device identifiers
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Analytics or tracking

📊 AdMob Usage (Free Version Only)

AdMob is used only in the free version and follows Google's privacy policy. See Google Privacy Policy for details.

Pro Version: 0 trackers, 0 internet permissions, 100% offline.

🔗 Link Processing

  • ✅ Links are processed locally on your device
  • ✅ No external services used for link analysis
  • ✅ Your browsing patterns remain private

📤 Data Export

When you export your link history, data is saved directly to your device using Android's Storage Access Framework. No data is transmitted to external servers.

🛡️ Permissions

  • INTERNET: Only for AdMob ads in free version
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check connectivity for ads
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: For battery monitoring
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: For app notifications

📞 Contact

For privacy questions, contact us through the app's About section.
