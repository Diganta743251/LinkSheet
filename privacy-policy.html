LinkMaster Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 29, 2025

Privacy First Promise: LinkMaster collects NO personal data. All links are stored locally on your device.

Data Collection

  • Free Version: Uses AdMob for advertising (Google's privacy policy applies)
  • Pro Version: Collects zero data - no analytics, no tracking, no internet permissions
  • Link Storage: All your links are stored locally on your device only
  • No Cloud Sync: We never upload your data to our servers

Attribution

LinkMaster is based on LinkSheet by Diganta Tarafder, available at https://github.com/LinkSheet/LinkSheet under MIT License.

Third-Party Services

  • AdMob (Free Version Only): Google AdMob may collect device identifiers for advertising purposes. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Firebase, or any tracking services

Permissions

  • Internet Access: Only for AdMob in free version
  • Storage: To save your link preferences locally
  • App Usage: Optional - to sort apps by usage frequency

Data Security

  • All data encrypted in transit
  • Local storage uses Android's secure storage APIs
  • No server-side data storage

Your Rights

  • All data stays on your device
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Export/import settings locally

Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: privacy@linkmaster.app

Open Source: LinkMaster is open source. Verify our privacy claims by reviewing the code on GitHub.
